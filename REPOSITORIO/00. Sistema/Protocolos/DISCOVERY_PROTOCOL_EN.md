# 🛸 App Blueprint Generator — Discovery Protocol: Your Vision, Our Technology

👋 **Before you start (very important)**

This document is **not technical**. You don't need to know how to program, understand artificial intelligence, or have used complex digital tools.

👉 You only need one thing: **to know your reality.**

We are not going to build the application yet. We are going to do something much more important: **think carefully about what you really need.** Because if this is not clear, the technology doesn't help, automation fails, and time and money are lost.

🎯 **When you finish this document, you will have:**
*   Total clarity on your real problem.
*   A structured idea for the solution.
*   A solid base for the **Antigravity** engine to build your application correctly.

🧠 **How you should respond:**
*   **Respond as you speak:** Don't look for perfect answers or try to use technical terms.
*   **Be clear and honest:** This document is designed to understand you, not to evaluate you.

## 🟢 BLOCK 2: The Real Problem (Here is the Value)
⚠️ **This is the most important block.**
**🎯 Objective:** To detect what really deserves to be solved to save you time.

1.  **The "Robot" Task:** What do you do repeatedly that takes up your time?
    *   **Your answer:** "I spend hours doing ___________________________".

2.  **The Moment of Error:** In what part of the process do you usually fail or do problems arise?
    *   **Your answer:** "I usually have failures when ___________________________".

3.  **What part of your job is the most frustrating?**
    *   *This is usually where the golden opportunity to automate lies.*
    *   **Your answer:** _________________________________________________

4.  **Real Impact:** If this problem disappeared tomorrow, how much time would you save per month and what would change in your life?
    *   **Your answer:** I would save ____ hours and my day would be ___________________.

## 🟢 BLOCK 4: Your Ideal Solution (Your Desire)
**🎯 Objective:** To imagine the final result without thinking about "how" it's done.

> [!NOTE]
> **Technical note:** What goes into the app (like an email) we call **Input**, and what comes out (like a PDF or notification) we call **Output**.

1.  **If you had a perfect digital assistant, what would it do for you?**
    *   *Complete the phrase:* "When [EVENT] occurs, I want the application to automatically [DO THIS] and then deliver [THIS RESULT] to me."

2.  **What things do you want to keep controlling personally?**
    *   *It is vital to know what the machine should NOT decide on its own.*
    *   **Your answer:** _________________________________________________

3.  **How do you want to "see" the solution?**
    *   *Note:* We call this the **Interface (UI/UX)**.
    *   [ ] Something very visual and with bright colors.
    *   [ ] Something serious, clean, and office-like (tables, lists).

## 📚 Little Dictionary for your Project

| Technical term we will use | What it means in real life |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Blueprint** | The "master plan" or DNA of your application. |
| **Skill** | An "ability" that our AI already has (e.g., sending WhatsApps). |
| **Input / Output** | What "goes in" to the app and what "comes out" manufactured. |
| **Dashboard** | Your main control panel, where you see everything at a glance. |
